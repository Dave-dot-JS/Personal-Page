About

Hi. Welcome to the first iteration of my personal catch-all page! This is where you can find general information about me and the many things I do. But by way of introduction, I'm Dave路JS, and I am a guy that does a whole lot of stuff. Borderline too many stuffs. By day, I work as a Senior Actuarial Analyst, where I have over 5 years experience working with major P&C Insurers in the Toronto Area. It's about as interesting as it sounds.


But by night, I am the drummer for Toronto music projects Sixteen Scandals and The Sun Harmonic. Fun stuff right? I've got a drumming vlog you can check out here.


I am also passionate about programming. Most recently, I have been finding ways to combine this passion with my background in statistics to break into the world of Machine learning. Even with multiple courses and certificates already under my belt, I am a non-stop learner and am always looking for ways to apply my new-found skillset to new projects or tasks. In time, I hope to fully transition into a Data Scientist/Machine Learning Engineer role in downtown Toronto.


Thought one blog was enough? Think again! I also keep a personal blog tracking my journey into Machine Learning and programming in general (okay mostly machine learning) here.


Oh, I also do some web development. I made this site! It's not terrible! I'm also pretty into tabletop wargames, RPGs, and martial arts. You could say that my real hobby is collecting hobbies.
