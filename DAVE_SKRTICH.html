About Me

I am a Business graduate from the University of Western Ontario, with a specialization in Finance. For the past five years, I have been working in the actuarial field of the insurance industry.

In 2018 I decided it was time for a change and began pursuing my long-time interest in coding. I have successfully completed a 10-week Front-end Web Development course offered by Bitmaker General Assembly, and have developed a strong foundation in HTML5, CSS, and Javascript. My goal is to continue expanding my knowledge and ultimately get a job as a full-stack developer at a small to mid-size startup company in Toronto.
