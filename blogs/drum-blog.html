Welcome to my drumming blog! In summer of 2018, in nan effort to force myself into practicing more, I made a committment to learn one new cover-song per month on drums, and post my progress along the way. This is where all of those posts go! In addition to practice posts, I will also share clips from live shows and from the recording studio with my band Sixteen Scandals
A quick disclaimer: I am not here to teach, I am here to learn. If documenting my own learning can help someone else with their own journey, all the better. But that's not the primary function of this page. And that being said, constructive criticism is most welcome! If you have feedback or suggestions for future songs to cover, just hit the Contact button above and shoot me a message!
